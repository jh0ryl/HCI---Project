About the Creators

Welcome to our waste segregation game, an interactive and educational app designed to teach players the importance of proper waste management. The game challenges users to sort trash into four key categories: residual, recyclable, organic, and hazardous, promoting awareness of environmental responsibility while making learning fun and engaging. Developed by BSCS 3-2 students, this project highlights the practical application of Human-Computer Interaction principles to create an intuitive and user-friendly experience that inspires positive ecological habits.
